Summary:A simple and configurable goal tracker.
Description:
A simple tool to keep track of and record measurments in pursuit of your goals. Goals can be
created and edited.
Several goal types are available:
* Single values like weight measurements.
* Cumulative values such as time spent practicing or distance walked.
* Periods of Daily, Weekly, or Monthly, or named days during the week.
